Fitness for Everyone

FitStar puts convenience, control, and the change you want to see in yourself back in your hands. Want to get flexible, strong, lean, or just want to get moving? Our yoga or personal trainer apps—with more on the way—will craft your perfect routine and challenge you when you've hit a plateau.

Watch the video
Your workouts are your own

FitStar designs your personalized workouts based on how you're progressing. Each Session, each Move, each repetition is constantly adjusted based on your input. When it starts to burn, you have yourself to thank.

Friends and Family Keep you going

Cheer each other on or, if you feel like raising your intensity and the level of competition, start a challenge! Choose between minutes per month, calories burned, or total number of sessions, then name your stakes. Winner takes all!

Connected to the big picture

Wearable technology, wifi-enabled body scales, fitness trackers; the internet of things is only getting bigger and FitStar is at the heart of it all, helping you gather, access, and interact with all of your own data, with all the best devices.

Anytime. Anywhere.

We all want to live a healthy life but struggle to find the time to get in a good workout. If you’ve got 7 minutes, you’ve got time for a FitStar session. We’re always on your schedule.

Think this screen is wide?

Airplay FitStar applications to your AppleTV for a true widescreen experience. Bigger is sometimes better.

The Transit Challenge

The next time you're on the subway or bus, try balancing without holding on to anything. It's great for your core!
